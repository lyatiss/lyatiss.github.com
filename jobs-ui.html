Job Description: Front End Engineer

At Lyatiss we are changing the way users leverage the power of Cloud Infrastructure as a Service by improving drastically their experience, generating outstanding performance and stimulating exceptional creativity. Nice to Have

  • Experience with Backbone or similar frameworks
  • Mobile & tablets development using HTML 5.0 or native iOS & Android
  • Visualization of large data sets (Computer Network, Maps.)
  • Experience in usability engineering and testing
  • Proven track record of successful web designs (Portfolio)
